The Artist

Canada's Leading
Indian Crossover DJ

DJ Harshid is one of Canada's leading Indian crossover DJs and a driving force behind the new wave of global club sound. Based in Vancouver, he has built a powerful reputation through explosive sets that fuse Bollywood, Punjabi, techno, bass, and global club music into one high-impact experience.

With over 6 million global streams and a rapidly growing international audience, Harshid has toured across major cities including Toronto, Vancouver, and Calgary — bringing his signature high-energy production to packed venues and festival crowds.

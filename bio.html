The Artist

Harshid

DJ · Bollywood Fusion · Electronic

Where the East meets the Electric

Harshid is one of Canada's leading Indian-crossover DJs — blending Bollywood, Punjabi, Hip-hop, and global club sounds into electrifying live sets that move the room.

With over 6 million global streams and a rapidly growing international audience, Harshid has toured across major cities including Toronto, Vancouver, and Calgary, bringing his signature high-energy production to packed venues and festival crowds.

Known for his jaw-dropping stage presence and genre-blending style, Harshid creates immersive culture-crossing sets that feel both from the heart and from the future. Cutting-edge production has made him a go-to artist for promoters looking to deliver lineups that keep dance floors packed all night long.

"I don't just play music — I build a moment."

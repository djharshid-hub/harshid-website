Artist Overview

Harshid is one of Canada's leading Indian-crossover DJs, blending Bollywood, Punjabi, Hip-hop, and global club sounds into electrifying live sets. With over 6 million global streams and a rapidly growing international audience, Harshid has performed across Canada's major cities and continues to push the boundaries of South Asian club culture.
